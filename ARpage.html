J.Murray Inc. 1944 Sign

This is a sign of the company J.Murray Inc. This was a Spring-making company in the UK during WW2 that also helped in making uniforms and helmets for soldiers. Nowadays the company is run by the grandson of J.Murray and they make WW2 style war helmets.

What you can do

There are multiple features on the Kelham Island AR website what you can do whilst walking around the museum.

These features include:

  • View different objects inside the museum in Augmented reality.
  • Scan QR codes around the museum to bring up AR objects.
  • Read and learn about the museum and what it beholds.

Why I made this website

This website was made as an improvement to Kelham Island's original Site. This is because their old site looked boring and needed a more engaging improvement

Therefore, I have created this in order improve the interactivity whilst walking around the museum.
